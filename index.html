Why Join Us?

Dublin Coding Club was set up with you, the member in mind. We wanted to build a club that would encourage those who, perhaprs had never even tried coding to come and try, all the way up to the experienced coder who would like to meet like minded members with a passion for coding. We hold classes 3 times a week to accomidate all skill levels, from the complete beginner, through to the more advanced, we can cater for all. Our tutors have combined experience of over 30 years in coding!

We currently have over 2000 members and we would love you to be one of them. So come on down to one of our classes every Monday Wednesday and Friday and get coding!




What Will you learn?

You'll start with some basic HTML or markup for your first few classes, advancing later to CSS and Java script for our more experienced members. We have a class to suit all.

As your skill level grows, we grow with you. We always strive to push you to that next step in coding, weather you're a keen beginner or a dedicated coding lifer!
